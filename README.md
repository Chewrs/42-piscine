# 42 Piscine 2026 🏊

This repository contains all my work from the 42 Piscine, which I attended from 4 May 2026 to 29 May 2026.

## What is 42?

42 is a global tuition-free programming school with campuses around the world. Instead of traditional classes and lectures, students learn through projects, peer-to-peer collaboration, and problem solving. The school is open to people from all backgrounds and focuses on developing both technical skills and the ability to learn independently.

➡️ [Learn more about 42](https://42network.org/)

## What is the Piscine? 
The Piscine (French for "swimming pool") is a four-week intensive selection program for students applying to 42. During these four weeks, candidates learn by completing projects covering basic shell commands and C programming, from the fundamentals to recreating simple system utilities. Along the way, they take exams and work both independently and with their peers through peer evaluations and collaboration.

## My Experience

I attended the Piscine right after finishing high school with no experience in low-level programming languages.

During these four weeks, I had to learn quickly, manage my time, and collaborate with others. The Piscine gave me a deeper understanding of programming fundamentals, but more importantly, it exposed me to people from many different backgrounds, each with their own way of learning, thinking, and solving problems. That perspective was one of the most valuable things I gained from the experience.

If you are interested in computers, programming, or seeking a unique challenge, I highly recommend considering the Piscine. It is an experience you will not forget.
